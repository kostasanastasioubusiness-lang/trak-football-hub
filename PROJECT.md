## Kill Criteria (from Requirements)

- **Player-first thesis killed:** <30% of pilot players log weekly by
  end of week 8 → pivot to coach-first wedge or abandon Trak.
- **Triangle thesis killed:** ≥50% of pilot coaches stop assessing after
  week 4 → the product cannot sustain on player logging alone.
- **Parent role retired:** <20% of invited pilot parents ever open the
  app during the 8-week window → remove parent role from MVP scope.

## Scope Guardrails (Hold Scope)

Do NOT build:
- Training log, standalone wellness, highlights, CV export
- 1-on-1 requests, attendance, coach progress dashboard, child switcher
- Push notifications, email notifications (beyond auth)
- Payment, pricing, subscription
- Marketing site, App Store listing
- Desktop layout, native apps
- Algorithm tuning beyond v1
- Any feature not in PRD.md
